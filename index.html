Phase 1 Case Study: User Empathy & Early Leadership

Situation: Customers faced complex issues requiring escalation, while the team needed leadership during a period of transition.

Task: Resolve high-stakes complaints efficiently, improve underlying processes, and supervise a team of 15 to handle escalations effectively.

Action: Personally handled critical fraud/legal cases. Designed a new, more efficient policy flow for chargebacks. Led 1:1s, coaching, and development plans to upskill the team, and acted as the voice of the customer in new process pilots.

Result: Reduced turnaround time for chargeback claims, increased team performance and autonomy, and ensured customer feedback was integrated into new process designs, preventing future issues.

Phase 2 Case Study: Business & Finance Logic

Situation: High volume of overdue debtors posed a significant revenue risk, and inefficient reconciliation processes created inaccuracies in financial reporting.

Task: Reduce loss exposure from bad debt, improve the speed and accuracy of financial reconciliations, and streamline collection processes.

Action: Implemented targeted collection strategies and partner outreach programs. Drove process improvements for dispute resolution (e.g., B.open 2.0). Collaborated with Accounts Receivable to streamline allocation logic.

Result: Successfully reduced loss exposure while balancing partner satisfaction. Significantly improved reconciliation speed and data accuracy, creating more reliable financial data for decision-making.

Phase 3 Case Study: Product Partnership

Situation: As the company scaled, the global billing process (+12M invoices/year) faced risks of revenue leakage, compliance breaches (SOx), and needed to support new product lines.

Task: Optimize billing processes for accuracy, ensure robust compliance controls, and act as a key finance stakeholder for new product launches.

Action: Used SQL/Hadoop for deep-dive data analysis to identify and prevent discrepancies. Owned multiple SOx controls, partnering with Risk to strengthen them. Actively co-designed new billing flows with Product & Tech, contributing requirements and test scenarios for launches like Flights and Packages.

Result: Implemented preventive actions that significantly reduced revenue risk. Strengthened the compliance posture of the billing platform and ensured new products launched with robust, scalable billing logic from day one.

Phase 4 Case Study: Leadership & Strategy

Situation: A payment delay after an SAP migration created an urgent need for a ~â‚¬30M partner compensation solution. Separately, ongoing operational inefficiencies were a drag on resources.

Task: (1) Design and launch a compliant compensation flow under extreme time pressure. (2) Systematically translate operational pain points into product features to drive long-term efficiency.
